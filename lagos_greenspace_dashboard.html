Key Finding
The city's poorest residents live in its hottest, least green zones.

Lagos has one of the lowest urban green space ratios of any megacity in the world. But the deficit is not evenly distributed. High-density, low-income mainland LGAs have up to 9√ó less green space per person than wealthier island areas ‚Äî compounding heat stress, air quality issues, and mental health impacts on the most vulnerable residents.
